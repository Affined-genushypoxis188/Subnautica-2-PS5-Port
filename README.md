## 🛠️ Troubleshooting Common Issues

If you encounter problems, follow these solutions to resolve them.

**The application fails to start**
Ensure you have the latest drivers for your graphics card. Visit your manufacturer website and download the most recent update. If the issue persists, run the application as an administrator by right-clicking the .exe file and selecting "Run as administrator."

**Low frame rates**
Lower the graphical settings in the in-game menu. Turn off motion blur and depth of field to reduce the load on your hardware. Ensure no heavy programs run in the background while you play.

**Controller problems**
The application supports standard PC controllers. Connect your gamepad before you launch the program. If the game does not detect your controller, check your Windows settings under Devices and Printers to confirm a successful connection.

**Missing files warning**
If you see a warning about missing files, your antivirus software may have flagged the installer. Check your antivirus history and restore the file if it was marked as a threat. We recommend adding the game folder to your antivirus whitelist to prevent future interference.

**Slow loading times**
Verify that you installed the game on an SSD. If you installed it on an external USB drive, moving it to your internal drive often corrects performance issues.
